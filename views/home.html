What is BioSIM

Biometric Student Information Management system (BioSIMÂ©) is a web based School Information Management System that has automated students' and school information management in Kenyan schools.

BioSIM Performs the following functions

  • Uses the iris as the unique biometric identifier
  • All students will be enrolled into the system
  • These involves the capture of each eye, digital facial photo and Biodata
  • A student biometric file is then created using this information
  • Roll call is performed by the teacher when students are in class
  • The students look into the iris scanner and the information is sent to the server via mobile network confirming that the student is indeed present.
  • At the same time parent receive SMS messages on their phones informing them that their children are in class.
  • Parents will also be enrolled and tracked during PTA meetings
