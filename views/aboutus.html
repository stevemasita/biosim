ABOUT BioSIM

BioSIM© provides the ability to track the following:

  1. Enrollment
  2. Students attendance
  3. Educational performance
  4. School fees payment history
  5. Teacher attendance
  6. Parents attendance during school meetings
  7. Overall school performance (OSCARating) based Ministry of Education Science and Technology Quality Index rating system for each school
The BioSIM Sytem

BioSIM System Comprises of:

  1. Live scan enrollment station comprising of dual or single Iris scanners, Android phone, laptop computers and BioSIM system. These will be used to capture iris impressions of all participants as well as their head shots.
  2. Verification system comprising a single iris scanner and android phone. This will be used by the teachers to scan the students’ iris while in class and will be linked to the student information management system where it will have the time and date stamps of when the scan happened. This is effectively the time and attendance system.
  3. A central repository with all students, teachers, parents’ and school data that can be remotely accessed by any authorized person.
  4. OSCARating system for Ministry of Education Quality Department

School Performance

  1. BioSIM offers the Ministry of Education Science and Technology with the entire Quality Index rating tool.
  2. This integration offers the Quality Assurance and Standards Officers with a one stop platform that assess schools and generates a report real time.
  3. The platform is able to link the school performance to the quality index school, this break through integration will allow for the alignment of resources to schools with great need.
